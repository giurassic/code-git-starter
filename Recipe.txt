Bacon Egg and Cheese Sandwich

You need:
A few slices of Bacon
1 Big fat egg
A few slices of cheese
2 Half of one loaf of bread
Fuck tomatoes

Recipe:

Heat up pan with medium heat
Put in the bacon and wait until its done
Take out bacon and put it on a paper towel
Use the fat from the bacon to cook the egg in the same pan
Take out the bacon and lightly toast the loafes on the pan
Put the cheese, bacon and egg on the loafs and let them toast until the cheese melts
